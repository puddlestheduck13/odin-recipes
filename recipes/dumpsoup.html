Dumpling Soup

Description

Dumpling Soup - best made with Trader Joe's Gyoza Potstickers

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger, miced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 c chicken stock
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 large carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • frozen dumplings (potstickers or mini potstickers)
  • 2 c fresh spinach, packed
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a soup pot set over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and saut√© until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add chicken stock, soy sauce, green onions, carrots and salt to the pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the dumpling (potstickers) to the pot and bring back up to a boil. Cook for 4 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in spinach.
  6. Serve topped with sesame seeds and fresh green onions.
