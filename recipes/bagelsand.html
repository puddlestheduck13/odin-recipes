Breakfast Bagel Sandwich

Description

Easy homemade bagel recipe from scratch, made in the air-fryer!

Ingredients

For Bagels

  • 1 c flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 c plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • "Everything But The Bagel" seasoning

For Sandwich

  • Eggs, cooked over-easy
  • Bacon
  • Kraft Singles American Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Whisk in bowl.
  2. Add yogurt, mix with fork until well combined.
  3. Lightly dust flour on work surface. Knead the dough until tacky, ~20 turns.
  4. Divide into 4 equal balls. Roll into 3/4-inch thick ropes, join ends to form bagel.
  5. Brush with egg wash, add seasoning to both sides.
  6. Air fry @ 280 for 15 minutes or until golden.
  7. Slice, top with egg, bacon and cheese
