Nan's Banana Bread

Description

This is Nan's World Famous Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 1/4 c sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 1/2 c flour
  • 3/4 c chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine butter and sugar. Add eggs.
  2. In seperate bowl, combine sour cream and baking soda.
  3. Mix with sugar mixture.
  4. Add mashed bananas along with flour.
  5. Add vanilla and chocolate chips.
  6. Let mixture stand 1/2 hour, add to greased baking pan.
  7. Bake @ 325 for ~1 hour
